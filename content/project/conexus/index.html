For Conexus I work as an external advisor on one of their products - the Entrepreneurship Mindset Tool. This instrument is a game-based assessment used to measure entrepreneurial skills from the European EntreComp Framework. This assessment will be mainly deployed in three African countries: Uganda, Tanzania and South-Sudan.
